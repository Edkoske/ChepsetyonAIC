About Chepsetyon AIC Bethel

Our History

Chepsetyon AIC Bethel was established in 1985 by a group of committed believers who felt called to start a Bible-believing church in this community. What began as a small gathering under a tree has grown into a vibrant congregation of over 500 members.

Throughout our history, we have remained committed to preaching the pure Word of God, serving our community, and raising up disciples who love Jesus Christ. We are part of the larger Africa Inland Church denomination, which has a rich history of missionary work and church planting across East Africa.

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ who love God, love people, and transform their communities through the power of the Gospel.

Our Vision

To be a Christ-centered church that is known for biblical teaching, authentic worship, compassionate service, and effective evangelism in Chepsetyon and beyond.

Core Values

  • Biblical Authority: We believe the Bible is God's inspired and authoritative Word
  • Worship: We worship God in spirit and truth
  • Fellowship: We are committed to authentic Christian community
  • Discipleship: We invest in growing mature followers of Christ
  • Service: We serve God by serving others
  • Evangelism: We share the good news of Jesus with our community

Statement of Faith

We believe in:

  • One God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit
  • The deity and humanity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, miracles, substitutionary death, bodily resurrection, and ascension
  • The Holy Spirit who convicts, regenerates, indwells, and empowers believers
  • The Bible as the inspired, infallible Word of God
  • Salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone
  • The church as the body of Christ
  • The personal return of Jesus Christ
